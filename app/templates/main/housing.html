Housing

Housing is broken down into two different endpoints, rooms and buildings. Rooms has a bunch of room data (gasp) as well as some lottery information... Buildings has a list of amentities... We don't offer joins across these two datasets, so get creative! ;)

Endpoint housing/rooms

GET Request

http://data.adicu.com/housing/rooms?is_suite=true&api_token=API_TOKEN
